I am an associate professor at Japan Women's University specializing in intellectual history and political theory, with particular focus on Jane Addams, Hull House, and Progressive Era social reform movements.

## Research Interests
- Jane Addams and pragmatist feminism
- Progressive Era social reform
- Democratic theory

## Current Projects
I am currently writing a book about Hull House and Jane Addams's philosophy of "Ethical Democracy" for general audiences.
